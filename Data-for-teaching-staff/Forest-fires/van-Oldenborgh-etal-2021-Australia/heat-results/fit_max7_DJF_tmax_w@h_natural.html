This is the Fraction of Attributable Risk (FAR) due to the trend. In order to interpret it as the FAR due to climate change a quantitative argument has to be added that connects the trend to anthropogenic factors, via a climate model or a scaling argument to a temperature trend that already has been attributed. The uncertainty estimate does not include the structural uncertainty in the statistical model due to the assumptions made on the previous page, so the true uncertainty is larger.
