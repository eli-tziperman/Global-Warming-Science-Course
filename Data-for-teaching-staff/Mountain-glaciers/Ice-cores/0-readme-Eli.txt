I used the NOAA paleo search site (downloaded from the NOAA World Data Service for Paleoclimatology Paleo Data Search Web Service) to find all ice cores by Lonnie Thompson, from 60S-60N, 180W-120E
https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/paleo-search/

here is the search link:
https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/paleo-search/study/search.json?dataPublisher=NOAA&dataTypeId=7&minLat=-60&maxLat=60&maxLon=120&investigators=Thompson%2C%20L.G.&headersOnly=true

From the many files downloaded and available under the folder data/, I extracted the Quelccaya Summit Done (QSD) data from two papers:

Thompson et al 2013 (Science),
Thompson et al 2017,
contained in files

quelccaya2013.txt,
quelccaya2017qic1976d18o.txt
quelccaya2017qic2016d18o.txt

to this directory, to use it for the analysis in the notes.
